About Us:


Since 1981 the Echo organization has been pairing animals with families and individuals who are looking to share their home. Our mission is to find animals "forever homes" where they can be part of a family for generations.


We have close partnerships with organizations that work with senior citizens in order to help pair our pets with members of our community with love to give. We are a nonprofit organization and we work to insure that animals are given all the chances to be adopted.
